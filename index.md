# Privacy Policy for Nully

**Last Updated:** April 28, 2026

---

## Introduction

Welcome to Nully ("the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Nully, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

Nully is a video editing application. The content you create (videos, projects, edits) is stored **locally on your device**. We do not upload, transmit, or store your video files, audio files, or project data on any external servers.

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

We use third-party services that may collect certain information automatically, including:

- **Firebase Analytics:** Collects anonymous usage data such as app opens, feature usage frequency, session duration, device type, operating system version, and general geographic region. This data helps us understand how users interact with the App and improve the experience.

- **Firebase Crashlytics:** Collects crash reports, stack traces, and device state information when the App encounters an error. This helps us identify and fix bugs. Crash data may include device model, OS version, and the state of the App at the time of the crash.

- **Firebase Remote Config:** Used to manage feature flags and app configuration remotely. No personal data is collected through this service.

### 3. Advertising Data

The App uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data for personalized or non-personalized advertising, including:

- Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
- IP address
- General location data
- App usage information

For more information, see [Google's Advertising Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

---

## Device Permissions

Nully requests the following device permissions to function properly:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Storage (Read/Write)** | To access, import, and save video and audio files on your device |
| **Camera** | To record video content directly within the App |
| **Microphone** | To record audio for video projects |
| **Internet** | To display ads, send crash reports, and fetch remote configuration |
| **Foreground Service** | To continue video processing tasks while the App is in the background |

These permissions are used solely for the stated purposes. We do not access your files, camera, or microphone beyond what is required for the App's functionality.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- **Improve the App:** Analyze usage patterns to prioritize features and improvements
- **Fix issues:** Identify and resolve crashes and bugs through crash reporting
- **Display advertisements:** Serve ads through Google AdMob to support the App
- **Configure the App:** Deliver feature updates and configuration changes via remote config

---

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We share data only with the following third-party service providers, strictly for the purposes described above:

- **Google Firebase** (Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config) — [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **Google AdMob** (Advertising) — [AdMob Privacy Policy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

These providers are governed by their own privacy policies and comply with applicable data protection regulations.

---

## Data Retention

- **Local data** (your projects, videos, exports) remains on your device until you delete it.
- **Analytics and crash data** is retained by Firebase according to their standard retention policies (typically 60 days for Crashlytics, 14 months for Analytics).
- **Advertising data** is managed by Google AdMob per their data retention policies.

---

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

- All video and project data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers
- Communication with third-party services (Firebase, AdMob) uses encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- We do not maintain any databases containing your personal information

---

## Children's Privacy

Nully is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

---

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Opt out of personalized ads:** You can adjust your ad preferences in your device settings
- **Reset your advertising ID:** Available through your device's privacy settings
- **Delete local data:** You can delete all projects and app data through your device's app settings
- **Request information:** Contact us to learn what data, if any, we hold about you
